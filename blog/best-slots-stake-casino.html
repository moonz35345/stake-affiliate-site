5 Tips for Playing Slots on Stake

💡 Tip 1: Check RTP Before Playing

Always look for slots with 96%+ RTP for better long-term value. You can usually find the RTP in the slot's info or help menu. Stake doesn't always display RTP in the lobby, so check inside the game.

💡 Tip 2: Understand Volatility

High volatility = bigger but rarer wins (need larger bankroll). Low volatility = smaller but more frequent wins (better for bonus wagering). Match the volatility to your bankroll and goals.

💡 Tip 3: Use Bonus Buy Wisely

Many Pragmatic Play slots on Stake offer a "Bonus Buy" feature where you can pay 100x your bet to jump straight to the free spins round. This can be profitable on high-RTP slots but is extremely risky. Only use it with money you can afford to lose.

💡 Tip 4: Set a Budget and Stick to It

Decide your bankroll before you start and never exceed it. Slots are designed to be entertaining, not profitable. If you're chasing losses, take a break.

💡 Tip 5: Try Demo Mode First

Most Stake slots let you play in demo mode without depositing. Use this to learn the bonus features and volatility before betting real money.
