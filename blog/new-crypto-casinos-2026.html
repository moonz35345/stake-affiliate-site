How to Check if a New Casino Is Safe

Before depositing at any new crypto casino, check these safety signals:

  • Valid gaming license — Look for Curaçao, Malta (MGA), or Isle of Man licenses. Avoid completely unlicensed casinos.
  • Provably fair games — If the casino offers provably fair originals, it shows commitment to transparency.
  • Reputable game providers — Casinos featuring games from Pragmatic, Evolution, NetEnt, etc. are more trustworthy than those with unknown providers.
  • Player reviews — Check forums, Reddit, and Trustpilot for real player experiences.
  • Test the withdrawal — Make a small deposit, play a bit, and withdraw. If the payout comes through quickly and without issues, that's a good sign.
  • Responsive support — Test customer support before depositing. Slow or unhelpful support is a red flag.
